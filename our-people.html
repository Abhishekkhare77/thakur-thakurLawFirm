Ms. Akanksha Tiwari Singh


Ms. Akanksha Tiwari Singh completed her law degree in 2018 from Lloyd Law College, Delhi NCR. Following her graduation, she joined Integreon Managed Solutions in Noida, working as an Attorney Associate. After gaining initial experience, Akanksha returned to Raipur, Chhattisgarh, and joined Pransh Law Firm, where she specialized in arbitration. Seeking further career growth, Akanksha relocated to Hyderabad and worked for one year under Senior Advocate Krishnan Grandhi.


During this time, she appeared in the High Court of Telangana, as well as in DRT (Debt Recovery Tribunal) and NCLT (National Company Law Tribunal) proceedings. Currently, at Thakur & Thakur Associates, Akanksha is a practicing advocate with over four years of extensive experience in dispute resolution. She is part of a commercial litigation and arbitration team, regularly handling matters related to domestic arbitration, MSME, NCLT, and DRT. Additionally, she possesses a comprehensive understanding of laws related to the banking laws.
